Who We Are

More Than an Event Company

Sree Saaj Events & Caterers is based in Kayamkulam, Kerala — a company built with a single mission: to make every celebration as special as the memories it creates. What started as a small wedding catering venture has grown into a full-service event management powerhouse serving all of Kerala.

Today, our team of dedicated professionals handles everything from intimate family gatherings to grand corporate galas — always with the same meticulous attention to detail and heartfelt care.

We are proud to be the trusted event partner for families, businesses, and institutions across Kerala, bringing authentic traditions and contemporary elegance together. Call us at +91 8075707566 or +91 9447334172.
